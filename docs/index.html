About Wheated

Fine Dining Heritage

Our Vision

Wheated brings authentic Neapolitan tradition to Brooklyn with a modern twist. Founded in 2018, we're dedicated to crafting exceptional wood-fired pizzas using imported Italian ingredients, creating innovative craft cocktails, and curating a world-class whiskey collection.

Every pizza is made with San Marzano tomatoes and aged mozzarella di bufala. Our mixologists craft each cocktail with precision, and our whiskey sommelier personally selects rare bottles from distilleries worldwide. We believe exceptional dining is about quality, tradition, and experience.
